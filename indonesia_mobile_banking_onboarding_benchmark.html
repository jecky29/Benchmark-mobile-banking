🏆 Digital-native banks dominate — Jenius, Blu, and Allo Bank occupy the top 3 slots. Their greenfield tech stacks allow full API-driven onboarding with no legacy branch workflows bleeding into the app experience.
⚠️ Credit card onboarding lags CASA by ~18 points on average. Income verification, credit bureau pulls (SLIK/OJK), and card-courier logistics add friction that digital-native players have partially solved via instant virtual cards.
✅ eKYC coverage is near-universal for CASA (14/20 full, 5/20 partial) but patchy for credit cards. State-owned banks (BNI, BRI, Mandiri) require branch visits or video-call verification for CC, adding 2–5 days to approval timelines.
📱 Auto-fill & OCR gaps — only 9 apps offer KTP OCR pre-fill for both products. Typing a 16-digit NIK manually on mobile is a documented drop-off point; apps that removed it show 12–22% higher completion rates in published case studies.
💳 Virtual card issuance is the key differentiator for CC onboarding. Apps that issue a virtual card immediately on approval (Jenius, Allo, TMRW) score 15+ points higher on the CC journey than those requiring physical card delivery.
